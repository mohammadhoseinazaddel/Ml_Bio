Deep Neural Networks Can Predict New Onset Atrial Fibrillation From the 12-Lead ECG and Help Identify Those at Risk of Atrial Fibrillation–Related Stroke

<div dir="rtl">

-	 A-fib بی علایم یا دارای غلایم کمی است
